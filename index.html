HOW IT WORKS
Pure in 3 Steps
01

Fill With Any Tap Water

The activated carbon filter immediately begins removing chlorine, odors, and common contaminants as you pour.

02

Press to Purify

Tap the cap button or trigger UV-C purification from the app. The UV-C LED sterilises the full water volume in 60 seconds.

03

Drink & Track

The bottle tracks every sip, syncs to your phone, and adjusts your hydration goal in real-time based on activity and weather.
